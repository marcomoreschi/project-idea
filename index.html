About Us

Speedy Fit has been designed with every fitness requirement and schedule in mind. With the community at the heart of it’s recent refurbishment, the space provides facilities for all abilities to take advantage of personal training sessions, classes or simply as a space to work out in your own time. In the modern cardio room, you will find a traditional range of gym equipment along with some high-tech surprises. There’s a Skill Mill, for treadmill-style athletic workouts, Stairmaster, for incline HIIT programmes and a Watt Bike Pro and Assault Air Bike – for competitive and recreational cyclists alike. Alongside the cardio room there is a generously equipped weight training area (with cathedral ceiling) and upstairs you will find a superb disco-lit spin studio, with the latest Keiser Indoor Cycles. The Fitness Management team, led by Marco Moreschi have designed a diverse range of classes including HIIT, Box Fit, Body Blast Circuit, Ab Attack, and Spin. What’s more, membership of the gym is not required to take advantage of personal training sessions or the comprehensive class timetable.
